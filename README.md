## 📌 Project Overview

Insurance companies operate in a highly competitive environment — processing thousands of customer complaints while managing substantial premium volumes. This Power BI capstone project analyzes automobile insurance data across **223 insurance companies** to uncover meaningful patterns and deliver **actionable business intelligence** for strategic decision-making.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

The automotive insurance sector faces three core challenges:

| Challenge | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| 📊 **Data Volume** | Thousands of customer complaints processed annually across multiple carriers |
| 👁️ **Performance Visibility** | Need for clear insights into company performance metrics and complaint resolution effectiveness |
| 🏆 **Market Intelligence** | Understanding premium trends and competitive positioning across the industry |

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

| # | Objective | Description |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 01 | **Revenue Analysis** | Comprehensive analysis of total premiums written across all insurance companies |
| 02 | **Complaint Evaluation** | Detailed assessment of customer complaints and resolution categories to identify service quality trends |
| 03 | **Performance Ranking** | Identification of top-performing insurance companies based on premium generation and complaint metrics |
| 04 | **Trend Tracking** | Monitoring premium trends across different years to understand market dynamics and growth patterns |
| 05 | **Strategic Insights** | Delivery of actionable insights to support business decision-making and competitive positioning |

## 📂 Dataset Description

### Data Structure
The dataset contains comprehensive automobile insurance company data spanning multiple years.

**Key Attributes:**
- `Company Name` — Name of the insurance provider
- `Premium Written` — Total premium revenue generated
- `Complaint Counts` — Number of customer complaints filed
- `Filing Year` — Year of data submission
- `Performance Ranking` — Ranking based on key metrics

### Data Preparation
Extensive **data cleaning and transformation** were performed in Power BI:
- ✅ Handling missing values
- ✅ Standardizing company names
- ✅ Calculating derived metrics (e.g., complaint ratios)
- ✅ Building relationships between tables for dynamic filtering

## 🔑 Key Insights

### 📈 Company Performance
```
Total Premiums Generated      →  87K
Average Complaint Ratio       →  26.45%
Market Leader                 →  GEICO General Insurance
```

- **GEICO General Insurance** emerges as the dominant player with the highest premium generation
- **Market concentration** is significant — a small number of companies control the majority of market share, indicating strong barriers to entry
- Premium distribution reveals clear **competitive advantages** among established players

### 📋 Complaints Analysis
```
Total Companies Analyzed      →  223
Total Complaints Recorded     →  ~36,000 cases
Trend                         →  Decreasing after peak periods
```

- A substantial proportion of complaints are **not upheld**, indicating possible customer dissatisfaction with claim decisions
- Complaint trends show **fluctuations across years** but generally decrease after peak periods — suggesting improved claims processing or market stabilization
- High complaint volumes highlight areas for **service quality improvement**
